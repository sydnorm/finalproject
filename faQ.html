Frequently Asked Questions
q: what should I bring to my first session?
a: come prepared with comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and any questions or goals you’d like to discuss during your consultation.
q: i’m new to working out. can I still sign up?
a: absolutely! my training services are designed to meet you at your current fitness level, whether you’re a beginner or an experienced athlete.
q: how do I know which service is right for me?
a: if you’re looking for individualized attention, personal training is a great fit. for a more social experience with friends or teammates, group personal training or sports camps might be the better option. Feel free to reach out for guidance!
q: are sessions tailored to my goals?
a: yes! every session is customized to align with your specific fitness and wellness goals, whether that’s building strength, improving endurance, or enhancing sports performance.
q: do you offer virtual training sessions?
a: at this time, all training sessions are conducted in person in Buies Creek, NC.
q: how often should I schedule sessions?
a: this depends on your goals and availability. many clients train 2-3 times per week to maintain consistency and see steady progress.
q: can I split a training package with a friend?
a: for group personal training, each participant pays individually ($25/hour per person). for personal training, packages are intended for individual use.
q: do you offer nutrition coaching?
a: while I don’t provide formal nutrition plans, i can offer general nutrition tips and guidance to complement your fitness journey.
q: what’s the cancellation policy?
a: cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid being charged for the session.
q: how do I book a session?
a: click the contact button below to book your consultation or package today!
