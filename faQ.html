Frequently Asked Questions
What should I bring to my first session?
Come prepared with comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and any questions or goals you’d like to discuss during your consultation.
I’m new to working out. Can I still sign up?
Absolutely! My training services are designed to meet you at your current fitness level, whether you’re a beginner or an experienced athlete.
How do I know which service is right for me?
If you’re looking for individualized attention, personal training is a great fit. For a more social experience with friends or teammates, group personal training or sports camps might be the better option. Feel free to reach out for guidance!
Are sessions tailored to my goals?
Yes! Every session is customized to align with your specific fitness and wellness goals, whether that’s building strength, improving endurance, or enhancing sports performance.
Do you offer virtual training sessions?
At this time, all training sessions are conducted in person in Buies Creek, NC.
How often should I schedule sessions?
This depends on your goals and availability. Many clients train 2-3 times per week to maintain consistency and see steady progress.
Can I split a training package with a friend?
For group personal training, each participant pays individually ($25/hour per person). For personal training, packages are intended for individual use.
Do you offer nutrition coaching?
While I don’t provide formal nutrition plans, I can offer general nutrition tips and guidance to complement your fitness journey.
What’s the cancellation policy?
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid being charged for the session.
